    Data Sources

    Time series from countriesâ€™ National Statistical Offices (NSOs) are the fundamental units of the database. They include compositional data and structural analysis is based on the relationsships between these variables. In the process of selecting and mapping variables from national definitions and harmonizing national classifications of economic activities with the International Standard Industry Classification, these relationships need to be monitored closely. You will be able to identify relevant series from NSOs and combine them using basic estimation methods.
